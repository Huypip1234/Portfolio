Finding a

Meaningful life

Kristine Lorraine
For most of history, the meaning of life was not a topic of sustained reflection because it seemed uncertain and quite unpleasant to define. The new age begins with millennials. Have you ever stepped back to ask yourself questions on what we live for and what might be our future. Evitably, self-consciousness is a generational challenge as well as a prerequisite to create true happiness and fulfillment within any cells of mankind.

Actually, we understand so much more about what we were taught at school, but we still grapple with intellectual concepts of life's meaning and its ultimate end. Some might feel disconnected and depressed by trying to find their place. Centuries have shown what later gets called a successful enterprise – at the outset – is just an idea about what they want for themselves and others. By wondering what is counted to be meaningful in your eyes? Once it has been clearly named, your life would have turned upside down completely just by discovering your inner self. Everyone has their own definition on what their life truly means. There is no idealistic frame or standard for anybody, as long as you have a faithful belief that you would thoroughly satisfy with all of your decisions every time you make an introspection later on. Others cannot be relied upon to determine what will be meaningful to your very existence. The experiences you have gotten from all of the trials and tribulations can be the most solid foundation to identify what would be the mainstay of your life. Indeed, instead of ignoring what is inevitable, by all well-intentioned gratitudes, listen to the inner voice and build your own life value. I am convinced that meaning could light my way into miracles, and it happened to me as the only reason to stay alive from the past inner crises. Hitherto, my ideology was settled down by these foremost elements - the four that shape the meaning of my life.
Love
Work
|
Family
At the dawn of a frantic world, satisfying living begins with an insight into happiness. For years. I have built the measure of success for myself that in retrospect, I did have the worth time on Earth without regrets. A couple years ago. I started to realise I had nothing left but meaningless survival; no reason, no last words, no compassion, and another chance seemed to be out of my reach. By that time, I did fail by default. But it was gone. The moment I captured my set of core criteria, I began to strip away all of the inessential by the strong will that I finally controlled. Moreover, it helps me to encounter all of the crucial choices in life, those momentary pleasures, vain triumphs, enchanted things which make me fall out of my orbit were eliminated, leaving only what is truly important. In my perception, well-meaning life philosophy is just simply what I appreciate and an indispensable part of my life. It boosts my happiness, satisfaction, pride, and courage day in and day out. It suddenly dawned on me the ideas of life is: a happy family where I belong. a dream I cherished as a mission for my existence, a wish to build a long-lasting tie with my beloved one and an individual needs to be relentlessly fostered. These are valuable treasures keeping me moving straight forward without distraction or disorientation along the way. Had I really obtained anything by trading the value of my life, I might have found nothing but an empty mind and a lost soul. I supposed that the deepest cognizance of mine is the nucleus for everything I preserve and pursuit in long-term. In my early 20s. I discovered how incredibly fortunate I had been, to found what I grateful for, which lead me to enter one of the most important periods of my life. It was formed by four centrally significant components inside my world: Work, love, family and I.

A compromise was reached that the goal of my working has never been merely about eaming. I've come to realize what makes work valuable are freedom, challenges, connection, and prospects. Instinctively, this approach can be more fascinating than ever. Having lived through all criticism, misunderstanding. isolation and refusal; they still cannot keep me from moving forward. It is the power that enables me to get the most well-rounded preparation for my career. There is no point yearning for a job just to fill a void around the day without the deeply fit in my work principles. Perhaps, in some circumstances, work satisfaction has never been gifted, and ambitious targets need to be endeavored than ever. Truth be told, there are few profound experts on the significance of love than those who are bereft of anyone to love. It is deeply unlikely that we will ever find someone who fits perfectly with our envisions, then much of what we expect become unnecessary. Unwittingly, mutual incomprehension truly is the perfect reason for irresponsibility, betrayal, selfishness, and insensitivity from others. The German writer Goethe expressed bitterly: "No one has ever properly understood me, I have never fully understood anyone, and no one understands anyone else". To comprehend the value of each individual, we have to move past the logic of ourselves to preserve our love as well as any other relationships. While our status in the perception of others is extremely fragile, vice versa. family become the most secure support for us no matter how swiftly does life change. Nowadays, most of our lives are spent in the external world. Unfortunately, our time for family seem to be too little. Given how busy our life is, caring is the simplest thing we could do for our parents. Those vivid memories worth more than any abstinent luxuries they have received. Last but not least, a part of my life meaning is myself. It includes my health, belief, knowledge, principle, interest, habit as well as everything that belong to my inner soul. These individualized pieces of my puzzle that I'm striving to complete significantly contribute to create a unique and picturesque scene of the world I want to build. Therefore, personally, the more effort I put into cultivating myself, the more possibility I have to actualize my dream. Furthermore, with an explicit mastery of myself, I would have given others a stable reliance to rely on.

The greatest successes come from understanding the smallest. The meaning of your life is already within your reach. If not, probably you haven't evaluated its value right enough to feel pleased with your surrounding simplest. Instead of paying a high price for lack of self-consciousness about life-meaning, you can start exploring your infinite potential by expanding your horizons into your innermost treasure. Embarking on a subconscious adventure might not be easy, but its prize would be worth far beyond anyone imagination.
If you believe that every life has equal value, it might let you down someday. The reality isn't as romantic as the fairy tale you were told, in fact, some lives are considered to be valuable while others are not – it's inequity. But optimistically saying, all of us can decide whether we are worthy or not base on an amazing privilege is time. Everyone has the same amount of time but the way we use it will reflect our value precisely in the future.

As an exploding civilization, our pace of living has changed astonishingly. It's a "double-edged sword" issue, to whom can adapt perfectly will tum out to be successful; parallel, it puts the unfit into misfortune. Seen in that light, I came across that the biggest difference between two sides above comes from what called Time. It hides miracle behind since time is the only thing everyone has exactly the same and also the only thing none spends identically. Being conscious about the value of each day you live, how will you spend your days? Either leverage it as a priceless asset or waste it as a pointless item? But remember, your future is the rewards you paid with every single moment you are spending. One of the most contradictory facts is that even though we admit the importance of time, we still couldn't use them effectively as our expectation. Imagine someone who is exceedingly productive, each day of their life creates value and it's accumulated to achieve merit rewards for themselves. Day by day, the ones who been left by the general pace of social living would wonder whether life wasn't fair or they weren't endeavoring enough to have a fruitful harvest for their life after all. Despite all excuses will be given, you still couldn't deceit yourself that you have to responsible for your life - it has been created on your own. While most other conditions may find it hard to control, you still be the master of your time, so use it as wisely as possible. Time is the series of your days; thus, having a productive day is contributing to initiate an extraordinary life. As a consequence, we all need to build a helpful standard schedule for ourselves straight away. So, with your 24 hours per day, how will you allocate it into four time-bottles below effectively?
Work
Essential activity
Learn
Rest and entertainment
Theoretically, a bunch of beneficial timetables is given throughout schools and general media, but it's a matter of individuality. Each of us has our own vision and orientation, which means applying one standard on the daily schedule is nonsense. Quite hard to say whether which tasks are useful or useless in daily activities; it depends on our conception about life-meaning that decides which tasks are the best support to our future. Beneath the difference between rewarding endeavors and poisonous activities lies a vision of what we are pursuing. Therefore, immersing in which supposed to be outside of orientation would be seen as a waste that brings nothing but procrastination for what deserves to undertake at the front sight. In job-seeking challenges, time management is one of the paramount skill that makes someone is appreciated, hired, promoted, and rewarded. Besides, in person, it's the skill anyone all needs to keep their schedule in harmony, productive, accurate, and fluent. To deal with a reasonable timetable is to organize ourselves by a higher discipline into a frame of the priority goals. Faced with too many choices at one time could be puzzling. but having a list of supportive work that leads to our destination is the first compulsory step to get rid of a thousand other distractions. After setting up a foundation of what you are striving for, building a management system is the next step to control your time potently. Many tools would be helpful to systematize the series of your work, and in order to be more objective, some rewards and punishments could be applied as motivation along the way. By the power - you already have inside, lift a finger on what makes sense to your lives.

In a bid to trace back to my past, in many amusements and self-complicated hypotheses, my entire academic period was squandered. At once in later midnight, in a room corner, was the time I regretted for a long-lasted day with nothing had been accomplished. Until the middle of global shutdown, I was looking back at the path I've been passing and seriously envision the picture where it would lead me to; it was devastating. For sure, no one wants to live in there for entire life, and neither I. As another scene that I had painted for myself turned my biography to a new page. During the following days, I urgently planned my framework, listed goals, set up daily targets, composed discipline regulation, and built a personal report system. Most of these extraordinary endeavors aim to struggle against the default of tedious fate in the eyes of a liberalist. The myth of the time revolution in reality, wasn't a phenomenal breakthrough as same as the misapprehension of people. Nothing can be changed in a night, it's a whole relentless process of self-enhancement. Throughout the historic pandemic, I've spent all of my time studying and initiating good habits. A typical day of mine has arisen from 4:30 am till 9:30 pm, sorted into four main groups of time includes working, self-studying, essential activities, and leisure. Studying and working 12 hours per day is a slightly uncomfortable experience for the 20-year-old that I've had. In every early morning, I usually report every single task I completed yesterday circumspectly. At this point, in search of life's clear-sightedness, my particular line of work generally consists of strategic planning, self-studying, immersing in design, researching, ideas experiments, reading books, networking, and fostering personal interests such as writing or crafting. By taking advantage of excel, my time can be analyzed by the chart to notice if it was leveraged well enough yet to minimize downtime as lowest as possible. It worked – as just a beginning of my developing process. Gradually, the timetable would be changed better over time and end up with myriad good habits of a successful master in self-administration as well as time management.

Time lends its possessor all the power to reach almost everything they want. Since having time means having knowledge, experiences, wisdom, and even a zillion priceless other things in the universe that someone could trade their hard-working period to achieve. Notwithstanding, whenever time hasn't had the respect it deserved, it would deprive almost everything valuable to its owner. And perhaps: The Titans of all historical periods might have nothing when they haven't been successful; but certainly, they knew how to live deservedly with the limited time they had.
In the middle of adulthood upheaval, humankind has tinged with intense hierarchy by the authenticity in mindset. And despite the widespread scale of information, rather oddly, filling the large part of the world, those are apprentices of life yet always be self-righteous according to a pitiful shortsighted perspective. In fact, beyond anyone's total control, the world's operation has its own rules that shape the righteous mindset of humans.

Being the most intelligent creature on this planet, the possibilities of human brains seem to be unlimited. Science has pointed us as a mysterious part of the world - a unique formulation came in sorts of wisdom, adaption, imagination, and sentiment that we are inherent. Along with that, it is the power that enables us to perceive, empathize, cultivate, and develop to keep the society moving forward throughout million years past. Reflecting on your comprehension about surrounding aspects infers that: "Your life is only as good as your mindset". Given how sure this approach can be, the more expanding of our minds, the more advancing of our lives. Therefore, stretching the reach of viewpoint has never been urgently critical than right now. The thing is, where to start and how to do it? Ordinarily, some might suppose that the minds of geniuses are structured differently from the rest of people. But the truth is, they simply take everything more seriously than we do, and owning a great mind doesn't have any secret recipe reserved for the mastermind. Hence, no matter who you are, wishing for a pleasant or admirable life, giving a clear notion of mindset is fundamental to build your gratifying dream. Like any planted seed on Earth, the maturity in our perception customarily runs through four prime stages: sow the consciousness, water the learning process, trim the flawed conceptions, and fertilize an open-minded spirit.
In line with the development of this age, our generation has to deal with millions of problems coming from conflicting personal opinions. Unfortunately, with all energy and knowledge equipped, commoners prefer to allege and depose than empathize and forgive each other. By the impoverished intellectual backgrounds, neither of whom has elicited an appropriate solution of its fact. Regardless of truths, the instinct of human egocentrism doesn't admit any fault of one did, which comes to an awful causality that they would rather win disgracefully or even end of their relationship than consider right-mindedness together. Day by day, a poor mindset will gradually undermine the entire career, rapport, mental health, and virtue until they honestly accept the lesson from their mistakes. Furthermore, we're living in a correlative community - every single thought, word, behavior, and attitude of us does not only represent ourselves but also has an impact on others. This suggests that we should learn to be more humble and tolerant towards the various stances of people. It would always be wiser if we were in their shoes than our own, simply because it delivers a broad sight to see an issue from many different angles. Remembering how touchy-feely our mentality could be, is the best way to avoid the trap of self-justifying hazard. These may seem relatively abstract, but it's happening in tranquility before we could timely awaken. Vice versa, in the halo of a growth mindset, who possessed a clear-sightedness would have the root signs of delicateness, charisma, compassion, and a certain admiration. Additionally, far beyond any descriptions, one of the preeminent benefits of a profound intellect is how it motivates those who besides to jointly towards a better social life. Thereby, we could use our minds to think more thoroughly in every circumstance. Since our inevitable connection with the outside world, enhancing every single aspect of mindset is merely not only a personal choice but also a compulsory duty to anyone. It could be challenging - even so, with your intelligence and the education you have received, how would you spend it?

From the bottom of delusion valley on comprehension, an idiot has initiated a remarkable journey with a willing change status. During the pilgrimage to the treasure of right concepts, I've detected plenty of faults in my primitive ideology. It hallmarked on me: Whatever I extremely trust at the moment might turn out to be flawed at a different time or situation. And sometimes, walking out of the antiquated well-worn path is better than following the ideals of the majority. While life-meaning is a choice and time is a gift, then a righteous mindset is the crystallization of knowledge, experience, and cognizance throughout a human's lifetime. As a principle ahead of time, a great mind could have obtained no other way without all of those elements. It's an endless adventure. When I take a trip down memory lane, my neglected thoughts once more tapped. What I have passively learned from parents teaching, academic programs, standard doctrines, or predecessor's advice would never definitely be deep-rooted in my soul until it has been verified and concluded by reality experiences alone. In other words, none of the worthy fruition is complimentary. Becoming an autodidact is always the proudest thing of my life. I'm exploring the world with the highest curiosity one might have. By all sources of worldwide information, most kind of subject I need to learn is already published. And much of what I'm absorbing has slowly reinforced my point of view for years. This accumulation comes to the growth but also equal challenges that I have synthesized from those whom I admire most. Inside my hidden library, a host of irrational and exceptional ideas could be found effortlessly. Perhaps, having live through criticism and the closest's disparagement, part of the fragile ego has disposed of itself. Honestly, I'm always grateful for the hardships I faced, failures I overpassed, and miseries I endured in my life. Because it gave me a strong will besides myriad precious lessons that I would never learn in theory. At this point, when I nearly graduate - responsibility lies on my wheel, with the tremendous ambition, is to get a better mindset after day.

Of all time, the world stratifies the human race into different classes according to their level of mindset. When a person's worth is evaluated on their worldview, they belong to where they are supposed to be. Although everyone's starting point might not be the same; however, nourishing a righteous mindset is the thing everyone can accomplish in any circumstances. With the insightful understanding, life won't be as difficult as we used to think. In the end, being tired after noises outside, inner peace is what one needs to perceive the right direction.
